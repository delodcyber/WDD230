About Us

Bountiful Foods is a small business that specializes in offering locally-grown, organic foods to its customers. The company is based in Carlsbad, California, but it has multiple food trucks that operate across the coastal areas between Los Angeles and San Diego. While the company sources most of its produce directly from local farmers, it also partners with some national and international suppliers. Bountiful Foods caters to walk-in customers at its Carlsbad store and food trucks, as well as phone and online orders. The company's mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including made-to-order organic fruit drinks and fruit bags, to both the local community and tourists visiting the coastal areas. The theme of Bountiful Foods emphasizes the importance of healthy eating, organic produce, and convenience to ensure customer satisfaction..
